Our Story

More Than Just Water.
A Legacy of Purity.

"We don't just bottle water; we bottle trust."

— Vishal Kumar, CEO

Viv-Elion Beverages was established in 2023 with a singular, uncompromising vision: to redefine what "premium" means in the hydration industry. Located in the pristine aquifers of Sangrana, Punjab, our facility is not just a factory—it is a sanctuary of technology and hygiene.

While others focus on volume, we focus on virtue. Every drop that enters our bottles has been filtered through 8 stages of advanced purification, ensuring that when you open a Viv-Elion bottle, you are tasting water as nature intended it to be—untouched, pure, and life-giving.

Our automated bottling line ensures zero human contact from extraction to sealing. This is how we maintain the "Diamond Standard" of purity that our customers trust for their families.

Vishal Kumar

Under Mr. Kumar's leadership, Viv-Elion has grown from a local startup to a regional powerhouse in the beverage industry. His background in technology and operations has driven the adoption of world-class automation at our Sangrana plant.
