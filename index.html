How We Do It

The Science of Purity

01

Source Extraction

We source directly from deep underground aquifers in Sangrana, ensuring the base water is naturally rich in minerals before we even begin.

02

8-Stage Filtration

Our water passes through sand filters, carbon filters, reverse osmosis, and UV sterilization to remove every impurity while keeping the taste.

03

Automated Bottling

From washing to filling and capping, no human hand touches the bottle. This guarantees 100% hygiene and BIS compliance.
