Our Process

8-Stage Advanced Filtration

We use a "Zero Human Touch" policy. From purification to bottling, everything is automated.

1. Multi-Grade Filtration

Raw water passes through Sand Filters and Activated Carbon Filters to remove dust, odor, and color.

2. Reverse Osmosis (RO)

High-pressure membranes remove dissolved salts, heavy metals, and viruses, ensuring 99.9% purity.

3. Auto RFC Packing

Rinsing, Filling, and Capping happen in a sealed glass chamber. Ozonization keeps water fresh for months.
