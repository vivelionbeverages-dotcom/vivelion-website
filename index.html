Our Origins

Born in 2023: The Sangrana Promise

The journey of Viv-Elion began in the historic village of Sangrana, Sri Muktsar Sahib. Recognizing the critical gap between local water supply and international quality standards, Mr. Vishal Kumar embarked on a mission to bridge this divide.

Starting with a small but technologically advanced pilot plant, we focused intensely on R&D. We analyzed groundwater strata, tested filtration membranes, and collaborated with engineering experts to design a purification process that retains essential minerals while eliminating 99.99% of impurities.

Today, that pilot project has evolved into a fully automated industrial facility, serving thousands of retailers across the region.

The Sangrana Facility

Where technology meets nature.

Infrastructure

Our plant is designed according to global HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point) guidelines. The facility features epoxy-coated flooring to prevent dust accumulation, positive air pressure zones in the filling area, and advanced stainless steel (SS-316) piping for all water contact surfaces to ensure zero contamination.

Production Capacity

With a high-speed automated line capable of producing thousands of bottles per hour, we are equipped to meet the growing demand of Punjab. Our scalable infrastructure allows us to rapidly expand production for summer peaks without compromising on the 8-stage purification protocol.

Quality Assurance

The 8-Stage Protocol

01

Ozonation

Kills bacteria instantly.

02

Sand Filtration

Removes sediments.

03

Carbon Filter

Removes odor & color.

04

Reverse Osmosis

The core purification.

Following these steps, the water passes through Micron Filtration (05), UV Sterilization (06), and Final Ozonation (07) before automated filling (08). This rigorous process guarantees compliance with BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) IS:14543 requirements.

Environmental Responsibility

World Without Waste.

Our responsibility extends beyond the bottle. At Viv-Elion, we are pioneering a "Circular Economy" model in Punjab. We recognize that our planet's resources are finite, and we treat them with the respect they deserve.

We have implemented advanced rainwater harvesting systems at our Sangrana plant to replenish the groundwater levels, returning more water to nature than we extract.
