Our Story

More Than Just Water.
A Legacy of Purity.

Viv-Elion Beverages was established with a singular, uncompromising vision: to redefine what "premium" means in the hydration industry. Located in the pristine aquifers of Sangrana, Punjab, our facility is not just a factory—it is a sanctuary of technology and hygiene.

While others focus on volume, we focus on virtue. Every drop that enters our bottles has been filtered through 8 stages of advanced purification, ensuring that when you open a Viv-Elion bottle, you are tasting water as nature intended it to be—untouched, pure, and life-giving.

The Science

Uncompromising Quality Control

01

Source Extraction

We source directly from deep underground aquifers. This water is naturally filtered by layers of earth, providing a mineral-rich foundation that is free from surface pollutants.

02

8-Stage Filtration

Our proprietary filtration process involves Sand Filters, Carbon Activation, Reverse Osmosis (RO), and UV Sterilization. We remove contaminants at the molecular level.

03

Touch-Free Bottling

From the washing of the bottle to the final capping and sealing, the entire process is automated. No human hand touches the product, guaranteeing absolute hygiene.

Our Commitment

World Without Waste.

Our responsibility extends beyond the bottle. At Viv-Elion, we are pioneering a "Circular Economy" model in Punjab. We recognize that our planet's resources are finite, and we treat them with the respect they deserve.

We have implemented advanced rainwater harvesting systems at our Sangrana plant to replenish the groundwater levels, returning more water to nature than we extract. Furthermore, our PET bottles are 100% recyclable.
