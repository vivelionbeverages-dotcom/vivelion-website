Understanding the "Mineral" in Mineral Water.

Product Science • Dec 10, 2025

Many people believe that "pure water" means water with absolutely nothing in it. This is a myth. While we remove impurities, bacteria, and viruses, the essential minerals are what give water its taste and health benefits. Here is why Viv-Elion is superior to standard RO water.

1. Calcium (Ca)

Calcium is crucial for bone health and heart rhythm. Viv-Elion mineral water retains essential calcium levels that are often stripped away in standard home RO filters. Drinking mineral-rich water is an easy way to supplement your dietary calcium intake.

2. Magnesium (Mg)

Magnesium helps regulate muscle and nerve function. A deficiency in magnesium can lead to fatigue and muscle cramps. Our 8-stage filtration process ensures that the water isn't "dead water"—it is alive with the minerals your body craves.

3. Potassium (K)

Potassium is a key electrolyte that helps maintain proper fluid balance in the body. It is essential for hydration, especially during the hot summers in Punjab.
