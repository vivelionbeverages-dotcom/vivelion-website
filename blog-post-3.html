Minerals 101: What Are You Really Drinking?

Posted on December 5, 2025

Not all water is created equal. While tap water keeps you alive, natural mineral water helps you thrive. The secret lies in the microscopic minerals dissolved in every drop. Here is a breakdown of the "Big Three" minerals found in premium water and what they do for you.

Did you know? The "taste" of water actually comes from its mineral content (TDS). Pure distilled water has no taste at all!

1. Calcium: Not Just for Milk

We all know calcium builds strong bones, but did you know it is easier for your body to absorb calcium from water than from food? Drinking mineral-rich water is a zero-calorie way to support your bone health and muscle function.

2. Magnesium: The Stress Reliever

Magnesium is a superstar mineral. It helps convert food into energy, regulates your nervous system, and can even help reduce fatigue. If you often feel tired or stressed, a magnesium deficiency might be the culprit.

3. Potassium: The Balancer

Essential for heart health and maintaining proper blood pressure, potassium is an electrolyte that needs constant replenishing. It helps keep your body's fluid levels balanced, especially after a workout.
