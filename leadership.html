LEADERSHIP

Guiding Viv-Elion with a commitment to integrity, innovation, and the people of Punjab.

VISHAL KUMAR

Founder & CEO

Vishal Kumar is the Founder and CEO of Viv-Elion Beverages. Kumar, who established the company in 2026, has led the brand from concept to reality. He developed the brand's vision in 2024 and assumed the position of CEO upon the company's incorporation.

Kumar is leading Viv-Elion through its critical launch phase as a new contender in the hydration sector. Viv-Elion is a purpose-driven startup providing mineral-rich beverages that make everyday hydration safer, all while strictly adhering to BIS standards. The result is a locally rooted business creating value for the Sangrana community and its consumers.

Before the commercial launch, Kumar personally oversaw the facility's development phase from 2024 to 2025.

During this foundational period, he was responsible for site acquisition and infrastructure planning in Village Sangrana. Under his direct supervision, the facility implemented the proprietary 8-Stage Purification Protocol to ensure zero-compromise quality. Kumar also spearheaded the initiative to integrate sustainable practices early on, opting for recyclable packaging materials ahead of industry mandates. This role included navigating the rigorous PMEGP and BIS certification processes to ensure market readiness.

Kumar began his entrepreneurial journey in Sri Muktsar Sahib, driven by a vision to elevate local manufacturing standards. He transitioned from the digital services sector to the beverage industry, applying modern management efficiency to traditional manufacturing.

"To me, this is not just an industry. It is a duty. A duty to the land of Punjab and a promise to you that every drop is as honest as the soil it comes from."

Prior to founding Viv-Elion, Kumar gained experience in business management and digital strategy through his earlier entrepreneurial ventures in the technology education sector.

Kumar is the primary stakeholder of the Viv-Elion project and manages all strategic partnerships.

Kumar, who speaks English, Hindi, and Punjabi, is currently advancing his business education at Panjab University. He is a native of Sri Muktsar Sahib.
