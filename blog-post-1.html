The Future of Beverages

Innovating hydration with advanced projection technology.

Innovation

Published on Dec 5, 2025

At Viv-Elion, we aren't just bottling water; we are redefining how you interact with it. Our latest research into holographic interfaces suggests a future where your bottle doesn't just hold liquid—it displays your hydration stats in real-time.

Imagine a wearable device that projects your daily water intake directly onto your wrist, syncing perfectly with your Viv-Elion smart bottle. This is the technology we are building today.

Why Holographic Tech?

Screens are a thing of the past. Projection technology allows for a cleaner, more hygienic interaction that keeps you focused on your health without the distraction of a phone screen.
