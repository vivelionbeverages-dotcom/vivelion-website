At Viv-Elion, we aren't just bottling water; we are looking at how technology changes the way we drink. Imagine a bottle that doesn't just hold water, but interacts with you.

Projection Technology on Bottles

We are currently exploring concepts for "Smart Labels." Using micro-projection technology, future bottles could display:

  • Real-time purity levels of the water inside.
  • Hydration reminders synced to your smartwatch.
  • Customized messages for gifting or events.

Why Tech Matters

In a world of advanced tech, your hydration shouldn't be analog. While our current focus is on the purity of the water itself (using our 8-stage automated plant), our R&D team is constantly looking ahead.
