THE SANGRANA LEGACY

Purity is not found; it is engineered. Discover the story behind the water that is redefining hydration standards across the region.

BORN FROM THE SOURCE.

The story of Viv-Elion began with a simple observation by our founder, Mr. Vishal Kumar: pure, mineral-rich water was becoming a luxury rather than a necessity.

In the heart of Village Sangrana, we identified deep aquifers untouched by modern pollutants. But sourcing was only the beginning. We envisioned a facility that would bridge the gap between international quality standards and local accessibility.

Today, our facility stands as a testament to that vision—operating with a Zero Liquid Discharge model and delivering hydration that is as responsible as it is refreshing.
