2025 The Vision Takes Shape

The journey of Viv-Elion began in early 2025. In a market flooded with generic brands and questionable quality, Mr. Vishal Kumar identified a critical gap. The consumers of Punjab deserved better than "just water." They deserved hydration that was chemically balanced, micro-filtered, and ethically produced.

Rejecting the shortcut of rebranding generic water, Mr. Kumar chose the harder path: building a facility from the ground up. He selected the village of Sangrana, Sri Muktsar Sahib, not by chance, but after extensive geological surveys revealed deep, protected aquifers ideal for mineral extraction.

The Build Engineering Excellence

Construction of the Sangrana facility was driven by a philosophy of "Zero Compromise." We imported advanced filtration membranes and designed a proprietary 8-Stage Purification Protocol that exceeds BIS standards.

While other plants relied on manual labor for filling and capping—introducing the risk of human contamination—Viv-Elion invested in a fully automated, hands-free bottling line. This was a bold financial risk for a startup, but it was the only way to guarantee absolute purity.

Today Setting the Standard

In less than a year, Viv-Elion has transformed from a daring idea into a household name in the region. Our "Blue & Gold" bottles have become a symbol of trust. We are not just a business; we are a community partner, creating local jobs in Sangrana and setting a new benchmark for environmental responsibility through our rainwater harvesting initiatives.

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

When he established Viv-Elion in 2025, his goal was not merely to compete in the beverage market, but to disrupt it. He saw that the industry had become complacent, accepting mediocrity as the norm. He challenged this by implementing pharmaceutical-grade hygiene standards in a beverage facility.

"We do not sell water; we sell trust. Every bottle that leaves our factory carries my personal guarantee of safety and purity."

Under his direct supervision, Viv-Elion has achieved 100% compliance with BIS regulations and is currently pursuing international ISO certifications to prepare for global export.
