OUR HISTORY

The story of Viv-Elion began in 2023 with a simple observation: pure, mineral-rich water was becoming a luxury rather than a necessity. Mr. Vishal Kumar founded the company with a mission to bridge the gap between international quality standards and local accessibility.

Starting with a state-of-the-art pilot facility in Village Sangrana, we spent months testing groundwater aquifers. We rejected standard filtration methods in favor of an 8-stage purification protocol that rivals global giants.
