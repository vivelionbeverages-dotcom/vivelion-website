THE FOUNDATION

Empowering communities in Punjab through clean water initiatives and education.

OUR MISSION

The Viv-Elion Foundation is the philanthropic arm of our company. We believe that businesses thrive only when their communities thrive.

We dedicate 2% of our annual profits to:

  • Installing free clean water dispensers in rural schools.
  • Supporting local farmers with sustainable irrigation education.
  • Replenishing groundwater in Sangrana through advanced rainwater harvesting.
